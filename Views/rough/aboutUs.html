About Dynamic Solution

By.Dynamic Solution

Dynamic Solution was established in July 2020 under Government of Nepal Company Act 2063.The company aims to transfer knowledge and technologies in civil engineering industry among engineers and experts in and outside Nepal. We provide high quality consultancy services using latest technology at an affordable cost. We also aim to empower young engineer through R&D and educational training services.

Service Areas

Design and Consulting

  • * Commercial, public and residential building
  • * Roads and bridges
  • * Hydropower studies and development
  • * Retrofitting of existing structure
  • * Railway and metro
  • * Irrigation, water supply and sanitation

Educational and training services

  • * Bridge design both manual and software based (Midas Civil, CSI Bridge)
  • * Building Design software based (ETABS, SAP2000,SAFE,REVIT)
  • * Trail Bridge Design
  • * Road Design
  • * Finite Element Analysis using Ansys
  • * Industry oriented-Geotechnical Engineering and Solution

Research and Development

  • * Earthquake Engineering and Structural Dynamics
  • * Transportation Infrastructure
  • * River Training and Hydrology

About the Founders

Yogesh Bhattarai, Managing Director

Mr. Yogesh Bhattarai is the Managing Director of Dynamic Solution. Mr. Bhattarai completed his Masters from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee in the field of Structural Dynamics in 2016. He worked in L&T (Larsen & Toubro) for two years in the field of metro and railway. In the tenure of two year, he was involved in the Design of Railway structure (OHE, Portal Structure, Station Building, Transmission Line Design, Sub-station building).

From 2018 onwards, he returned to Nepal and worked in hydropower sector as Design Engineer at CSPDR-SINOTECH JV in association with Soil Test. He was involved in Planning, Design and Feasibility study of Upper Arun Hydropower Project along with preparation of Tender Document.

Pratyush Jha, Technical Director

Mr. Pratyush Jha is the Technical Director of Dynamic Solution. Mr. Jha completed his Masters from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee in the field of Structural Dynamics as a department gold medalist in the year 2017. He was a DAAD Scholar at TU Munich during 2016-17. He returned to Nepal and worked on various prestigious building design and retrofitting projects as Structural Engineer at Digicon Engineering Consult. He later served as Bridge Design Engineer at Local Roads Bridge Program (LRBP) during 2019-20 and National Structural Earthquake Engineer â€“ Individual Consultant at UN Department of Safety and Security Nepal during 2018-21. Presently, he is involved in consulting as well as research activities related to bridge engineering.

Mr. Bhattarai and Mr. Jha are one of the few persons to crack the prestigious Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) Exam for admission in Master Program in IIT. They are actively working towards their vision to uplift the Civil Engineering Practice and Profession in Nepal to international standard.
